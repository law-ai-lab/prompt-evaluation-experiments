# Prompt Experiment Example

## Task

Explain why sleep is important for health.

### Prompt A

Why is sleep important?

### Prompt B

Explain in simple language why sleep is important for human health. Provide three reasons.

## Evaluation

Prompt B produces more structured answers because it clearly defines:

* level of explanation
* number of points
* audience understanding

Prompt engineering can significantly influence response quality and usefulness.
